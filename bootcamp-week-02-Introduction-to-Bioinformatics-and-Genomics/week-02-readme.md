# 🧬 Week 2: Computational Genomics – From DNA to Metastasis

Welcome to **Week 2** of the Computational Biology Bootcamp!  
This week focuses on foundational topics in **genomics**, with special emphasis on **cancer genomics** and understanding the **key genetic markers** involved in disease processes.

---

## 📚 Overview

This week's sessions explore the following themes:

### 🔬 Computational Genomics: From DNA to Metastasis
- General overview of **bioinformatics and genomics**
- Understanding the central dogma: **DNA → RNA → Protein**
- Applications of genomics in research and clinical settings

### 🧪 Cancer Genomics & Key Genetic Markers
- Introduction to **cancer biology and genetics**
- Common **mutations and biomarkers** in cancer
- Examples of how genomic data is used in cancer diagnostics and treatment
